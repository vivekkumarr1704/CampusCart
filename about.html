About CampusCart

CampusCart is a student marketplace designed to simplify campus life. Students can buy and sell bicycles, purchase academic books, and rent premium clothes for events and functions. Our goal is to create a convenient and affordable marketplace where students can easily find essential items within their campus community. CampusCart helps students save money, reuse products, and access premium clothing without expensive purchases.
